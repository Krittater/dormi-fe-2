# Dormi — Product Context

## Register

App UI (product register). Design serves operational tasks, not marketing.

## Audience

- **Primary:** Dormitory/apartment owners and on-site admins in Thailand
- **Secondary:** Small property managers handling 10–200 rooms across one or more buildings
- **Usage context:** Office desk or counter, often during billing cycles; bilingual TH/EN (CN optional)

## Primary tasks

1. Manage apartments, rooms, room types, and tenants
2. Record meter readings and configure charges
3. Run billing periods and publish invoices
4. Track payments, finance (income/expense), and tenant deposits

## Tone

Professional flat — trustworthy admin tool (Stripe / Linear bar). Emerald brand accent; no marketing gradients on authenticated surfaces. Auth pages match dashboard vocabulary.

## Success criteria (UX)

- User always knows where they are (breadcrumb + nav active state)
- List pages share FilterBar + DataTable patterns
- Empty states teach the next setup step
- Power users: keyboard navigation (⌘K), export, bulk actions where safe
